Frequently asked questions

How much does Ignia cost?
Nothing. Every feature in the app is free. There is an optional tip if you want to support development — it unlocks no features.
How do I delete my account and data?
In the app: open Settings → Delete account. This permanently removes your account and all logged data. You can also email me and I'll do it for you.
How do I export my data?
Settings → Data → Download CSV exports your full log history.
I forgot my password.
Tap Forgot password on the sign-in screen and we'll email you a reset link. If you originally signed up with Google or Apple, use that same button to sign back in — there's no password on those accounts.
The barcode scanner won't open the camera.
Camera access was declined. Open iOS Settings → Ignia → Camera (or Android Settings → Apps → Ignia → Permissions) and allow camera access. Ignia only uses the camera to read barcodes; no photos are stored or uploaded.
My calorie target looks wrong.
Targets come from your height, weight, age, activity level and goal. Re-check them in Settings → Goals. Ignia also recalibrates your daily target over time from your real weight trend.
Does Ignia work offline?
The web app (ignia.fit) works offline and syncs when you reconnect. The mobile app needs a connection to sync between devices.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your logs are yours. Ignia does not sell data and shows no ads. See the privacy policy for specifics.
How do I report a bug?
Email the address above with a screenshot if you can. Bug reports are the fastest way to get something fixed.
